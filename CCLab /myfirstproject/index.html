Welcome to Camo-Caterpillar


This is an interactive piece where you can explore the nature of the catipillar. It lives in a forest environment and can easily camoflouge itself to protect against predators. Along with that, when you press certain keys you can have the catipillar grow or shrink.


What is the Camo-Caterpillar?

The camo-caterpillar is a unique insect that interacts with the sky to defend from any predators. You can only find it, floating near trees because it only eats tree leaves. Due to the fact that this caterpillar goes hand-in-hand with the sky, the clouds move in the same direction as it.


Interct with Camo

To make the caterpillar change color, press the space-bar. To make the caterpillar, incognito with the sky, click your mouse on the sky and it will match the color of the caterpillar.


Defend from predators

In order for the caterpillar to defend itself, he must adapt to a different size. Press Y for it to grow and Z for it to shrink.
